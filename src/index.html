The Open Academic Research Project (OASP) is an open-source project aiming to advance scientific research and discovery, promote technology that assists the scientific and academic communities, and make research available worldwide for the good of all humanity.

Our core principles

1

Collaboration, not competition, drives innovation.

2

AI plays a unique role in surfacing, analyzing, and indexing of information in millions of research papers and academic journals.

3

Cancer, global warming—we play an important role in advancing research and aiding breakthroughs in critical research areas.

Contributions

  • Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence

    Open Corpus

    Open access to Semantic Scholar’s corpus of 7.2 million research papers metadata and 6.1 million paper URLs.

  • Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence

    Science Parse

    A library for extracting metadata–titles, authors, abstracts, references, and mentions of the references–from scientific papers. JVM based and flexible enough to be incorporated into any project.

  • Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence

    Citeomatic

    A deep learning model for citation prediction. New citations and references are linked and suggested from uploaded research papers (Computer science and neuroscience currently).

  • Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence

    PDFffigures 2.0

    A software library for extracting figures from research papers online.
